GV Statement of Faith

2018-01-01

1. In the divine inspiration, authority and sufficiency of the Bible

2. That God exists eternally in three persons – Father, Son and Holy Spirit to whom equal honour is due

3. That Jesus Christ, unique Son of God, became man, being begotten of the Holy Spirit and born of the virgin Mary

4. That he died for our sins, was buried and rose again for our justification

5. That he ascended to the right hand of God and is now the all-sufficient High Priest of His people

6. That he personally will return to receive his people and judge the world in righteousness

7. That man has fallen and needs the Holy Spirit’s work in regeneration and sanctification

8. That man may be restored to repentance towards God and faith in the Lord Jesus Christ

9. That at death the soul of man does not cease to exist or become unconscious

10. That the dead will be raised either to life or condemnation and that the blessedness of the righteous and the punishment of the unrighteous will, alike, be eternal
