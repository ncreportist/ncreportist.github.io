Long Distance Relationships

2018-01-01
  1. We both know each other’s schedules and have set our expectations accordingly
  2. We trust each other
  3. We found a way to “hang out” remotely

Tips:

  • Schedule (1-2) phone calls a week via skype at specific times. If one can make it, rearange for another night that week.
    • Decide a time to have a long, uninterrupted/non-distracted chat (find a pub/coffee shop if house is full). This will work as a booster and will help you cherish each other’s choices and delights. I feel insecure if I find out there is more than one other person at the otherside and will be less trusting/open in my conversation.
    • Provides feeling of security to both parties
    • The onus is on the party who cancelled to set the new time
    • All other conversations (whatsapp/other phone calls) have no pressure to respond
    • Reduces the risk of resentment of feeling like the other party has no time to speak when you want to speak
    • We like each other because we both pursue interesting lives. If our communications get in the way of out daily lives then we will have little interesting stuff to say!
      • We like each other for our individual interests and passions - nursing, church, bible study, family, friends, career. I want you to be 100% present when your engaged in them.
    • Reduce the frequency to 1 time a week if it is distrupting life.
  • Both be 100% open. If there is something on our mind, don’t keep it in to conserve the other persons feelings. Harder in the short term, but better in the long run.
    • If you dont like something I said, tell me.
    • Dont lie. If we accidentally lie, don’t feel bad about confessing and correcting yourself. E.g. I will sometimes over exaggerate a story to make is seem more interesting that it is (you have 47 sisters right??)
    • Never use the silent treatment
  • When not in our schedules phone call, life is more important (unless something important comes up). If a text takes 2 days to respond to, either party should ne feel resentment.
  • Do house work while in conversation
  • Send postcards / letters
  • Go dating
    • Start a 2 person book club
    • watch a series
  • never go more than six weeks without seeing one another in person
  • Call and text each other during the day
    • This wont be possible with my workload. I would sometime turn my phone on airplan mode so I can focus on my work/study/friends.
